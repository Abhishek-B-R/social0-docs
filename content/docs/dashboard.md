## Overview

The Dashboard is where you spend most of your time in Social0. When you sign in, you land here. On the free tier you can only explore—you cannot connect accounts or post. With a paid plan you can open the Composer to create a post, go to Connections to add accounts, open Posts to see drafts and scheduled and published posts, change Settings, and open Billing. Everything is one or two clicks away.

## How to get to the Dashboard

1. Go to [social0.app](https://social0.app) and sign in (Google or email).
2. After sign-in you’re taken straight to the Dashboard (usually the Composer screen).

If you’re already signed in, click the Dashboard link in the header or go to [social0.app](https://social0.app).

## What you’ll see

- **Composer** — The main place to write a post and click Continue to pick platforms and publish or schedule.
- **Sidebar or menu** — Links to Create, Posts, Connections, Settings, Billing, and other sections.
- **Posts** — List of all your posts (drafts, scheduled, published). Open any post to view, edit, or retry.
- **Connections** — List of connected accounts. Add or disconnect accounts here.
- **Settings** — Profile, timezone, date format, queue slots, and other preferences.
- **Billing** — Your plan, usage, and upgrade or cancel.

## Tips

- Bookmark the Dashboard URL so you can jump back quickly.
- Use the Composer for the fastest path from idea to publish; use Create when you want to pick the post type yourself.
