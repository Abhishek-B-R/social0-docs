# Billing FAQ

**How do I upgrade?**  
Go to [Dashboard → Billing](https://social0.app/dashboard/billing) and choose a plan. You’re redirected to our payment provider (Dodo Payments); after success you’re redirected back and your tier is updated.

**What if my trial ended and posts failed?**  
If some posts failed with "Payment required", the dashboard shows a banner with a link to Billing. Upgrade to an active plan and you can retry publishing.

**How do I change or cancel?**  
Use the Billing page to change plan or cancel. Cancellation and data retention follow our [Terms](https://social0.app/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://social0.app/privacy).
