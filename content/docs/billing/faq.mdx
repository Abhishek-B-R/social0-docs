## Overview

This page answers common questions about plans, payment, and canceling. For full details, see our [Terms](https://social0.app/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://social0.app/privacy).

## How do I upgrade?

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Billing**.
2. Click the plan you want (Starter, Growth, or Pro). You can start with a **3-day free trial**-use the product fully, then your paid plan continues when the trial ends.
3. You’ll be sent to our payment provider to complete the purchase (or start the trial).
4. When payment succeeds (or the trial starts), you’ll be brought back to Social0 and your plan will update. If it doesn’t update right away, wait a few seconds and refresh.

## What if my trial ended and some posts failed?

If posts failed with a message like “Payment required,” it usually means your paid plan or trial ended. Go to **Dashboard** → **Billing** and upgrade or renew. Once you have an active plan, you can retry publishing those posts from your Posts list.

## How do I change or cancel my plan?

- **Change plan:** Open **Billing** and select a different plan. You may be charged or credited according to the change.
- **Cancel:** Use the cancel or manage-subscription option on the Billing page. You may be taken to our payment provider to confirm. After you cancel, you keep access until the end of your current billing period; then you’ll be on the Free tier and won’t be able to publish or schedule.

Refunds and data retention are described in our Terms and Privacy Policy.

## What about Teams if I’m on Starter or Growth?

Solo **multi-workspace** works on Starter and Growth. **Collaborative teams** (inviting admins/members) require **Pro**. Details: [Teams & Workspaces](/docs/dashboard/teams).

## Do invited teammates need their own Pro?

**No** — not to collaborate on the owner’s team. They need a paid plan only for their own Main / solo workspaces. If the **owner’s** Pro lapses, collaboration pauses for everyone until they renew.

## Tips

- Keep your payment method up to date so your plan doesn’t lapse.
- If you’re not sure which plan you’re on, check **Billing**-it shows your current plan and usage.

## Common questions

**Q: I was charged but my plan didn’t update.**  
A: Refresh the Billing page and wait a few seconds. If it still shows the old plan, contact support with your email and the date of the charge.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Refund policy is in our Terms. Contact support with your account details and reason.

**Q: What happens to my posts if I cancel?**  
A: Your drafts and scheduled posts stay in your account. You won’t be able to publish or schedule until you have an active paid plan again. How long we keep data is in our Privacy Policy.
