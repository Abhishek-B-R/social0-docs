## Overview

Social0 has four plan levels: **Free**, **Starter**, **Growth**, and **Pro**. On Free you can sign up and explore the dashboard only—you cannot connect accounts or post anything. Connecting accounts and posting (including drafts and scheduling) require a paid plan. You can start with a **7-day free trial** to use the product fully, then continue with a paid plan when the trial ends. Higher plans add more accounts, more tweets per month, and features like bulk tools and auto-repost.

## Plan comparison

**Free**  
- Sign up and explore the dashboard only.  
- You cannot connect accounts or post anything (no drafts, no scheduling, no publishing).  
- Use this to look around; upgrade to a paid plan when you’re ready to connect accounts and post.

**Starter**  
- Publish and schedule posts.  
- Up to **5** connected accounts.  
- About **300** tweets per month on Twitter/X.  
- No bulk tools, resurface, or auto-plug.  
- Good for individuals or small accounts.

**Growth**  
- Everything in Starter, plus:  
- Up to **15** connected accounts.  
- About **1,500** tweets per month on Twitter/X.  
- **Bulk tools** — upload and schedule many images or videos at once.  
- **Resurface** and **auto-plug** (re-post or boost posts, add plugs to high performers).  
- Good for serious creators and small teams.

**Pro**  
- Everything in Growth, plus:  
- Effectively **unlimited** connected accounts.  
- Same tweet limit as Growth (about 1,500/month on Twitter/X).  
- **Priority support**.  
- Good for agencies or heavy users.

Exact pricing is shown on the Social0 website and in **Dashboard** → **Billing**. Early-adopter pricing may apply.

## How to upgrade

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Billing**.  
2. Click the plan you want (Starter, Growth, or Pro). You can start with a **7-day free trial**—use the product fully, then your paid plan continues when the trial ends.  
3. You’ll be sent to our payment provider to enter payment details and confirm.  
4. When you’re done, you’ll return to Social0 and your new plan (or trial) will be active.

## Tips

- Start with Starter if you’re not sure; you can upgrade to Growth or Pro anytime.  
- If you hit the account or tweet limit, the app will tell you; upgrade to get a higher limit.

## Common questions

**Q: Can I switch plans later?**  
A: Yes. Go to **Billing** and choose a different plan. You may be charged or credited based on the change.

**Q: What counts as a “tweet” for the monthly limit?**  
A: Each post you send to Twitter/X counts as one tweet. Posts to other platforms don’t count toward that number.

**Q: Do I need a credit card to sign up?**  
A: No. You need a card (or other payment method) when you choose a paid plan. You can start with a **7-day free trial** to try the product fully; when the trial ends, you continue by paying for the plan you chose.

**Q: How does the 7-day free trial work?**  
A: When you pick a paid plan (Starter, Growth, or Pro), you can start with a 7-day free trial. Use the product fully—connect accounts, post, schedule. When the trial ends, your paid plan continues and you’re charged. You can cancel before the trial ends if you don’t want to continue.
