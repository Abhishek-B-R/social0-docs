## Overview

Social0 has four plan levels: **Free**, **Starter**, **Growth**, and **Pro**. On Free you can explore the dashboard only — you cannot connect accounts or post. Connecting and posting require a paid plan (or a **3-day free trial**). Higher plans add more accounts, more tweets per month, growth tools, and — on Pro — **collaborative Teams**.

## Plan comparison

**Free**

- Explore the dashboard only.
- No connecting accounts, drafts, scheduling, or publishing.
- No multi-workspace or teams.

**Starter**

- Publish and schedule posts.
- Up to **5** connected accounts.
- About **200** tweets per month on Twitter/X.
- **Solo multi-workspace** (organize connections; not inviteable).
- No collaborative teams, bulk tools, resurface, or auto-plug.

**Growth**

- Everything in Starter, plus:
- Up to **15** connected accounts.
- About **1,000** tweets per month on Twitter/X.
- **Bulk tools**, **resurface**, and **auto-plug**.
- Solo multi-workspace (still no collaborative teams).

**Pro**

- Everything in Growth, plus:
- Up to **50** connected accounts.
- Same tweet limit as Growth (about 1,000/month on Twitter/X).
- **Collaborative Teams** — invite admins and members, shared workspaces.
- Max **5** owned collaborative teams; max **15** teammates per team (excluding owner, including pending invites).
- **Priority support**.

List price for Pro is **$49/mo**. Early-adopter pricing may still show in the app. Exact prices: website and **Dashboard → Billing**.

Full product guide: [Teams & Workspaces](/docs/dashboard/teams) · [Workspaces board](/docs/dashboard/workspaces).

## How to upgrade

1. Go to **Dashboard → Billing**.
2. Choose Starter, Growth, or Pro (optional **3-day free trial**).
3. Complete payment with our provider.
4. Return to Social0 — your plan (or trial) is active.

## Tips

- Need to invite people? Upgrade to **Pro**. Need separate account sets alone? Starter/Growth solo workspaces are enough.
- If you hit an account or tweet limit, the app tells you — upgrade for higher limits.

## Common questions

**Q: Can I switch plans later?**  
A: Yes. Open **Billing** and choose another plan.

**Q: Do teammates need Pro too?**  
A: No — not to collaborate on the **owner’s** Pro team. They need a paid plan for their own Main / solo workspaces.

**Q: What counts as a “tweet” for the monthly limit?**  
A: Each post you send to Twitter/X. Other platforms don’t count toward that number.

**Q: How does the 3-day free trial work?**  
A: Pick a paid plan, start the trial, use the product fully. When it ends, the paid plan continues unless you cancel.
