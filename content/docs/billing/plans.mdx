# Plans

- **Free** — No connected accounts for publishing; you can sign up and explore.
- **Starter (Lite)** — Early adopter $6/mo. 5 accounts, 300 tweets/month (Twitter/X). No bulk tools, resurface, or auto-plug.
- **Growth** — Early adopter $19/mo. 15 accounts, 1,500 tweets/month. Bulk tools, auto-plug, resurface.
- **Pro** — Early adopter $33/mo. Effectively unlimited accounts, 1,500 tweets/month. Same as Growth plus priority support.

Plan limits (e.g. `maxConnectedAccounts`, `allowBulkTools`, `allowResurface`, `allowAutoPlug`) are enforced in the app and API.
