## Overview

Your subscription decides whether you can connect accounts and post at all (free = explore dashboard only), how many accounts you can connect, and whether you have access to bulk tools and auto-repost. You can start with a **7-day free trial** to try a paid plan, then continue by paying when the trial ends. See your plan, usage, and upgrade or cancel from **Dashboard** → **Billing**.

## How to open Billing

1. In the Dashboard, open the sidebar or menu.
2. Click **Billing** (or **Subscription**).
3. You’ll see your current plan, how many accounts you’ve connected, and (for some plans) how many tweets you’ve sent this month.

## What you can do on the Billing page

- **See your plan** — Free, Starter, Growth, or Pro, and what’s included.
- **Upgrade** — Choose a paid plan and complete payment (or start a 7-day free trial). You’re sent to our payment provider; when you’re done, you come back to Social0 and your new plan (or trial) is active.
- **Change plan** — Switch to a different paid plan (e.g. Starter to Growth).
- **Cancel** — Cancel your subscription. What happens to your data and access is described in our Terms and Privacy Policy.

## Tips

- If you just upgraded and the page still shows the old plan, wait a few seconds and refresh. Sometimes it takes a moment to update.
- Keep your payment method up to date so you don’t lose access by accident.

## Common questions

**Q: Where do I cancel?**  
A: On the Billing page, use the option to cancel or manage subscription. You may be taken to our payment provider to confirm.

**Q: What happens when my trial or plan ends?**  
A: If you don’t have an active paid plan, you’re on Free: you can only explore the dashboard—you won’t be able to connect accounts or post anything. You can still sign in and upgrade (or start a new 7-day free trial) to start again. See our [Terms](https://social0.app/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://social0.app/privacy) for details.
