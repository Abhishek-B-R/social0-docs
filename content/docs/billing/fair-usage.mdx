## Overview

To keep the service fast and fair for everyone, we apply **fair usage limits** on how many posts you can send to **Twitter/X** per month. Other platforms (LinkedIn, Instagram, etc.) do not have a monthly post limit—only Twitter/X does. Limits are per plan and reset each calendar month.

## Tweet limits by plan

- **Starter** — Up to **200** tweets per month on Twitter/X.
- **Growth** — Up to **1,000** tweets per month on Twitter/X.
- **Pro** — **Unlimited** tweets per month on Twitter/X.

Posts you send to other platforms (e.g. LinkedIn, Instagram, Threads) do not count toward this limit. Only posts that are published to Twitter/X count.

## How to see your usage

Go to **Dashboard** → **Billing**. Your current plan and “Twitter tweets this month” (used / limit) are shown there. When you approach your limit, we’ll show the same numbers so you can upgrade or wait until the next month when the count resets.

## Tips

- If you need more than 200 or 1,000 tweets per month on Twitter/X, upgrade to Growth or Pro.
- The counter resets at the start of each calendar month (UTC). Your usage is shown on the Billing page.

## Common questions

**Q: Do LinkedIn or Instagram posts count toward the limit?**  
A: No. Only posts published to Twitter/X count toward the monthly tweet limit.

**Q: What happens when I hit my limit?**  
A: You won’t be able to publish new posts to Twitter/X until the next month (when the count resets) or until you upgrade to a higher plan. You can still publish to other connected platforms.

**Q: Can I get a higher limit on Starter or Growth?**  
A: The limits are set per plan. To get more tweets per month on Twitter/X, upgrade to Growth (1,000) or Pro (unlimited).
