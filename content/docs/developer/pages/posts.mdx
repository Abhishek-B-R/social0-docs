## Overview

Your posts—drafts, scheduled, and published—live in the **Posts** section of the Dashboard. You can open any post to view it, edit it, publish it, retry a failed one, or delete it. Filter by platform or time and sort by newest or oldest to find what you need.

## Where to read more

- [Posts](/docs/dashboard/posts) — The main Posts list and what you can do there.
- [Post detail](/docs/dashboard/posts/[id]) — Opening one post, seeing status per platform, and using Edit, Retry, Post again, or Delete.
- [Drafts](/docs/dashboard/posts/drafts), [Scheduled](/docs/dashboard/posts/scheduled), [Posted](/docs/dashboard/posts/posted) — Filter views for each type.
- [Edit a post](/docs/dashboard/posts/[id]/edit) — How to change content or schedule before a post goes out.
