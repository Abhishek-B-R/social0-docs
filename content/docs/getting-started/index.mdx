# Getting Started

1. **Sign up** at [social0.app](https://social0.app) — use Google or email.
2. **Verify your email** (if you signed up with email).
3. **Choose a plan** (or skip and connect later).
4. **Connect your first account** — go to [Dashboard → Connections](https://social0.app/dashboard/connections) and connect a platform (e.g. Twitter/X, LinkedIn).
5. **Create a post** — open the [Composer](https://social0.app/dashboard/composer), write or paste content, add media if you like, and click **Continue** to pick platforms and schedule or publish.

No credit card required to start. You can connect accounts and create drafts on the free tier; publishing and scheduling require a paid plan (Starter, Growth, or Pro).
