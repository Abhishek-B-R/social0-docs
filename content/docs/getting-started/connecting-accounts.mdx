## Overview

Before you can publish or schedule posts, you need a **paid plan** and at least one connected social account. On the free tier you cannot connect accounts—you can only explore the dashboard. Once you’ve upgraded, go to **Dashboard** → **Connections** to connect your accounts. Each platform has its own sign-in flow; we only ask for the permissions we need to post and manage content for you.

## How to connect an account

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Connections**.
2. Find the platform you want (Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.) and click **Connect**.
3. You’ll be sent to that platform to sign in (if you aren’t already).
4. Approve the permissions Social0 requests (e.g. to post, read your profile).
5. You’ll be brought back to Social0; the account will appear in your list.

After that, when you create a post you can choose that account (and any others you’ve connected) to receive it.

## How many accounts you can connect

It depends on your plan:

- **Starter** — Up to 5 accounts.
- **Growth** — Up to 15 accounts.
- **Pro** — Effectively unlimited accounts.

If you’re at your limit, the Connections page will say so and you can upgrade from **Billing**.

## Connection status

On the Connections page you’ll see each account and its status. If a connection is expiring soon or has expired, we’ll show a warning. For most platforms we refresh connections automatically; if one fails, you may need to disconnect and reconnect that account.

## Tips

- Connect the accounts you use most first so you can start posting right away.
- If an account stops working (e.g. you changed your password on that platform), try disconnecting and reconnecting it.
- TikTok may show your creator name after connection; that’s normal.

## Common questions

**Q: Is it safe to connect my accounts?**  
A: We use each platform’s official sign-in. We only request permissions needed to post and manage content. We don’t see or store your password for those platforms.

**Q: Can I connect the same platform twice (e.g. two Twitter accounts)?**  
A: Yes. Connect one account, then connect again and sign in with the other. Each will appear separately when you create a post.

**Q: What if my connection says “Expired”?**  
A: Disconnect that account and connect it again. You’ll sign in to the platform again and we’ll get a fresh connection.
