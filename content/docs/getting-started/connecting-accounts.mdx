# Connecting Accounts

Go to **Dashboard → Connections** to connect your social accounts. Each platform uses its official OAuth flow; we request only the scopes needed to publish and manage content on your behalf.

- **Account limits** depend on your plan (Starter: 5, Growth: 15, Pro: effectively unlimited).
- **Token status** is shown per account (ok, expiring soon, expired). Some platforms (e.g. YouTube, TikTok) auto-refresh; others may need reconnection.
- **TikTok** — We may show creator username and nickname from the TikTok API after connection.

After connecting, you can select which accounts to use when creating or scheduling a post.
