## Overview

The Billing page shows **your** plan (Free, Starter, Growth, or Pro), how many accounts you’ve connected, and — for some plans — how many tweets you’ve sent this month. From here you upgrade, change plan, or cancel.

**Billing is always personal.** Even when you’re working inside a team URL (`/dashboard/teams/:teamId/...`), **Billing**, Settings, and API keys stay on your own account. Opening Billing does not wipe team membership — return to a team URL to keep collaborating.

Team collaboration (invites, shared workspaces) depends on the **team owner’s Pro** subscription — not each member’s plan. Members do **not** need their own Pro to post on the owner’s team.

## How to open Billing

1. In the Dashboard, open the sidebar or **More**.
2. Click **Billing** → [social0.app/dashboard/billing](https://social0.app/dashboard/billing).

## What you’ll see

- **Current plan** — Free, Starter, Growth, or Pro.
- **Usage** — e.g. accounts connected vs limit (Pro cap: **50**), tweets this month when applicable.
- **Upgrade / Change plan** — Payment is handled by our provider; you return to Social0 when done.
- **Cancel** — You keep access until the end of the billing period, then Free.

Pro list price is **$49/mo**; early-adopter pricing may still appear in the app.

## Plans and collaboration

| Plan | Connect & post | Solo multi-workspace | Collaborative teams |
|------|----------------|----------------------|---------------------|
| Free | Explore only | No | No |
| Starter / Growth | Yes | Yes | No |
| Pro | Yes (up to 50 accounts) | Yes | Yes (invite teammates) |

Details: [Plans](/docs/billing/plans) · [Teams & Workspaces](/docs/dashboard/teams).

## If you just upgraded

The page may show the old plan for a few seconds. Refresh. If you’re a team owner renewing Pro, reopen Teams — collaboration unpauses after the plan updates. Contact support with your email and payment date if it doesn’t.

## When Pro lapses (team owners)

- Teams UI shows collaboration **paused** + renew.
- Teammates lose permission to act on the team until you renew.
- New invites and accepts are blocked.
- You still use Main and your own workspaces for solo work.

## Tips

- “Payment required” on a post usually means the plan/trial ended — renew, then retry from Posts.
- Teammates who only collaborate don’t need to upgrade for the owner’s team; they upgrade for **their** Main / solo workspaces.

## Common questions

**Q: I’m in a team — whose plan am I on?**  
A: Posts and limits for that team use the **owner’s** plan. **Your** Billing page is still your personal subscription.

**Q: Do invited members need Pro?**  
A: **No** — not to collaborate on the owner’s team.
