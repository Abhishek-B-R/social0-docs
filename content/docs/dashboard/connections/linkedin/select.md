## Overview

When you connect LinkedIn, you’re asked to choose what to connect: your personal profile and/or one or more company pages. This happens on a selection screen right after you sign in to LinkedIn. You pick what you want, confirm, and then those accounts appear in Social0 so you can send posts to them.

## How to choose LinkedIn accounts

1. In Social0, go to **Connections** and click **Connect** for LinkedIn.
2. Sign in to LinkedIn when asked and approve the permissions.
3. You’ll be brought to a selection screen that lists your **personal profile** and any **company pages** you manage.
4. Turn on the ones you want to use in Social0 (e.g. your profile and two company pages).
5. Click **Confirm** or **Connect**. You’ll return to the Connections page with those accounts added.

You can connect more LinkedIn accounts later by going through the same flow again (e.g. to add another company page).

## Tips

- You need to be an admin or have the right role on a company page to see and connect it. If a page doesn’t show up, check your role in LinkedIn.
- Each connected profile or page will appear as a separate account when you pick where to send a post.

## Common questions

**Q: I don’t see my company page.**  
A: Make sure you’re an admin (or have the right role) on that page in LinkedIn. Only pages you can manage will appear.

**Q: Can I add another page later?**  
A: Yes. Go to **Connections**, click **Connect** for LinkedIn again, and sign in. You’ll see the selection screen; pick the new page and confirm.
