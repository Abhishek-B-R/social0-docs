## Overview

A text post is words only-no image or video. You write your message, pick which accounts get it, and publish or schedule. It works on Facebook, Bluesky, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and Threads.

## How to create a text post

1. Open the **Composer** and type (or paste) your text. Don’t add any images or video.  
   **Or** go to **Create** → **Text post**.
2. Click **Continue** (if you started in the Composer).
3. Select the accounts that should receive the post.
4. Click **Publish now** to post right away, or **Schedule** to choose a date and time.

Each platform has its own character limit (e.g. Twitter/X). We’ll show how much space you have as you type.

## Where text posts work

- Facebook
- Bluesky
- Twitter/X
- LinkedIn
- Threads

If you need an image or video, use **Image post** or **Video post** instead.

## Tips

- Keep an eye on the character counter if you’re posting to Twitter/X; we’ll trim or warn you if you go over.
- You can save as a draft and come back to publish or schedule later.

## Common questions

**Q: Can I add a link in a text post?**  
A: Yes. Paste the link in the text. Each platform will show it in its own way (e.g. as a preview).

**Q: Why don’t I see Instagram or YouTube for text?**  
A: Instagram and YouTube in Social0 are for image/video/collection or video only. Use another post type for those.
