## Overview

A video post is one video with an optional caption. You upload the video, add a caption if you want, pick which accounts get it, and publish or schedule. It works on Facebook, Bluesky, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Threads, YouTube, Pinterest, TikTok, and Instagram.

## How to create a video post

1. Open the **Composer** and add a video (drag and drop or click to upload). Add a caption if you like.  
   **Or** go to **Create** → **Video post** and upload the video there.
2. Click **Continue** (if you started in the Composer).
3. Select the accounts that should receive the post.
4. Click **Publish now** to post right away, or **Schedule** to choose a date and time.

We check that your video’s aspect ratio and length are supported. If something isn’t allowed (e.g. wrong aspect ratio), we’ll show a message so you can fix it.

## Where video posts work

- Facebook, Bluesky, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Threads, YouTube, Pinterest, TikTok, Instagram

## Limits you’ll notice

- **Length** — Most platforms have a max length (e.g. TikTok often under 10 minutes). Keep your video within those limits.
- **Aspect ratio** — Common ratios like 16:9, 9:16, 1:1, 4:3, 3:4 are supported. If your file doesn’t match, we’ll ask you to use a different file or trim it.

## Tips

- Vertical (9:16) works well for TikTok and Instagram Reels; horizontal (16:9) for YouTube.
- Big files can take a while to upload. Wait for the upload to finish before publishing or scheduling.

## Common questions

**Q: Can I add more than one video in one post?**  
A: For a single post with multiple videos, use **Collection**. For a series of posts (e.g. a thread), use **Thread**.

**Q: Why did my video fail to upload?**  
A: Check the file size and length for the platforms you chose. If you see an error about aspect ratio, use a supported ratio (e.g. 16:9 or 9:16).
