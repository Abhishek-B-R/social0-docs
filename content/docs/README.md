## Overview

These docs are for people who use Social0—the product that lets you create and schedule posts for all your social accounts from one place. They explain how to sign up, connect accounts, create each type of post, use scheduling and other features, and manage your plan.

## What's in here

- **Getting started** — Sign up, choose a plan (free = explore only; paid = connect accounts and post), connect your first account, and make your first post.
- **Platforms** — How to connect each network (Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.) and what to expect.
- **Post types** — How to create text, image, video, thread, and collection posts.
- **Features** — Scheduling, queue, bulk uploads, and auto-repost (where available).
- **Billing** — Plans, upgrading, and canceling.

Everything is written in plain language. If something’s missing or unclear, use the feedback link in the app or reach out to support.
