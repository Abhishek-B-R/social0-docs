## Overview

Social0 lets you write once and publish everywhere. You create your post in one place, pick which accounts to send it to, and hit publish-or schedule it for later. No copy-pasting, no switching between apps.

You can connect Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok, Bluesky, Threads, Facebook, and Pinterest. One dashboard. One composer. Your content, your schedule.

## How to get started

1. Go to [social0.app](https://social0.app) and sign up (Google or email).
2. Verify your email if you used email sign-up.
3. Choose a plan or skip for now-on the free tier you can only explore the dashboard; you cannot connect accounts or post.
4. Once you have a paid plan, open **Dashboard** → **Connections** and connect your first account (e.g. Twitter or LinkedIn).
5. Open the **Composer**, write or paste your post, add a photo or video if you like, then click **Continue** to pick platforms and publish or schedule.

No credit card needed to sign up. On the free tier you can explore the dashboard only-connecting accounts and posting (including drafts and scheduling) require a paid plan (Starter, Growth, or Pro). You can start with a **3-day free trial** to use the product fully, then continue with a paid plan when the trial ends.

## What you can do

- **Create posts** - Text, images, videos, threads, and collection posts.
- **Schedule** - Set a date and time; Social0 publishes for you.
- **Manage connections** - Add or remove accounts from the Connections page (per workspace).
- **Teams & Workspaces** - Organize accounts; on Pro, invite teammates. See [Teams & Workspaces](/docs/dashboard/teams).
- **See everything in one place** - All your posts (drafts, scheduled, published) live in the Posts list.
- **Build with the API** - Publish and schedule programmatically via the [REST API](/docs/api/quickstart).
- **Manage with AI** - Connect Claude, Cursor, or ChatGPT via the [MCP Server](/docs/integrations/mcp).
- **Use the CLI** - Publish from the terminal with [`social0`](/docs/integrations/cli).

## Tips

- Start with one or two connected accounts so you can see how posting and scheduling work.
- Use the Composer for a quick start-it suggests the right post type based on what you add.
- Your timezone in Settings is used for all scheduled times.

## Common questions

**Q: Do I need to pay to try Social0?**  
A: You can sign up and explore the dashboard for free. To connect accounts or post anything (including drafts and scheduling), you need a paid plan (Starter, Growth, or Pro). You can start with a **3-day free trial**-use the product fully, then continue with a paid plan when the trial ends.

**Q: Can I change or cancel my plan later?**  
A: Yes. Go to **Dashboard** → **Billing** to change plan or cancel. See [Billing](/docs/billing/plans) for details.

**Q: Where do I connect my accounts?**  
A: **Dashboard** → **Connections**. Click the platform you want and follow the sign-in steps.
