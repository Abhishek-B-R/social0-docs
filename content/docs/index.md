## Overview

The main Social0 website at [social0.app](https://social0.app) is where you learn about the product and sign in. If you’re not signed in, you’ll see the marketing page with features, pricing, and a **Get started** button. Once you sign in, that button becomes a link to your **Dashboard**.

## How to get into the app

1. Go to [social0.app](https://social0.app).
2. Click **Get started** (or **Sign in** if you already have an account).
3. Sign in with Google or your email and password.
4. You’ll land in the **Dashboard** (Composer) where you can create and schedule posts.

## Tips

- Use the same email you want to use for your social accounts so everything stays consistent.
- From the Dashboard you can explore the app. With a paid plan you can open **Connections** to add accounts and **Posts** to create and see your content. On the free tier you cannot connect accounts or post.
