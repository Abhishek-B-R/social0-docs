## Overview

You can connect your TikTok account to Social0 and publish images and videos. Connection is done from **Dashboard** → **Connections**. After you publish, TikTok may take a few minutes to process the post before it appears on your profile.

## How to connect TikTok

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Connections**.
2. Find **TikTok** and click **Connect**.
3. Sign in to TikTok when asked and approve the permissions.
4. You’ll return to Social0 with the account connected.

When you create an image or video post, you can select your TikTok account to receive it.

## What you can post

- **Image** — One or more images with an optional caption.
- **Video** — Video with optional caption.

## Limits you’ll notice

- **Processing time** — TikTok often needs a few minutes after we send the post before it shows up. If you don’t see it right away, wait a bit and refresh.
- **Video length** — TikTok has its own limits (e.g. 10 minutes max for some accounts). Keep your video within those limits.

## Tips

- Use the right aspect ratio for TikTok (usually vertical) so it looks good in the app.
- If a post doesn’t appear after a while, check your TikTok account and try disconnecting and reconnecting in Social0.
