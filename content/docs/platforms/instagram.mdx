## Overview

You can post to Instagram through Social0 by connecting an Instagram account that’s linked to a Facebook Page. We support images, videos, and collection posts. Connection is done from **Dashboard** → **Connections**.

## How to connect Instagram

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Connections**.
2. Find **Instagram** and click **Connect**.
3. You’ll be asked to connect (or choose) a **Facebook Page** that’s linked to your Instagram account. Instagram posting in Social0 works through that link.
4. Sign in to Facebook if needed and pick the Page that has the Instagram account you want.
5. Approve the permissions and you’ll return to Social0 with Instagram connected.

If you don’t have a Facebook Page linked to Instagram yet, set that up on Facebook/Instagram first, then come back to connect in Social0.

## What you can post

- **Image** - Single or multiple images with an optional caption.
- **Video** - Video with optional caption.
- **Collection** - Collection post (multiple images and/or videos in one post).

## Tips

- Only one Instagram account per connected Facebook Page. To use another Instagram account, connect its linked Page separately.
- Captions and hashtags work like on Instagram; add them in the post when you create it.
