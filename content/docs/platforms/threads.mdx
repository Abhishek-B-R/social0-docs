## Overview

You can connect your Threads account to Social0 and publish text, images, videos, threads (multi-post), and collections. Connection is done from **Dashboard** → **Connections**. Threads is part of Meta; you may sign in with your Instagram/Meta account.

## How to connect Threads

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Connections**.
2. Find **Threads** and click **Connect**.
3. Sign in with your Meta/Instagram account when asked and approve the permissions.
4. You’ll return to Social0 with the account connected.

When you create a post, you can select your Threads account to receive it.

## What you can post

- **Text** — Plain text posts.
- **Image** — One or more images with an optional caption.
- **Video** — Video with optional caption.
- **Thread** — Multi-post threads (multiple posts in a row).
- **Collection** — Multiple images or videos in one post (collection post).

## Tips

- Threads has its own character and length limits; the composer will keep you within them.
- Use the thread post type when you want a series of connected posts, and the collection type when you want one post with multiple images or videos.
