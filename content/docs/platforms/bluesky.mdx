## Overview

You can connect your Bluesky account to Social0 and publish text, images, videos, and threads. Connection is done from **Dashboard** → **Connections**. You’ll sign in with your handle and app password.

## How to connect Bluesky

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Connections**.
2. Find **Bluesky** and click **Connect**.
3. Enter your Bluesky handle (e.g. you.bsky.social) and an **app password**. You create app passwords in Bluesky settings (Settings → App passwords); don’t use your main account password.
4. Confirm and you’ll return to Social0 with the account connected.

## What you can post

- **Text** - Plain text posts.
- **Image** - One or more images with an optional caption.
- **Video** - Video with optional caption.
- **Thread** - Multi-post threads (multiple posts in a row).

## Tips

- Keep your app password private. If you ever change it in Bluesky, disconnect and reconnect Bluesky in Social0.
- Threads are posted in order; Social0 sends each part one after another.
