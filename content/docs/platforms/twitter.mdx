## Overview

You can connect your Twitter/X account to Social0 and publish text, images, videos, threads, and collections. Connection is done from **Dashboard** → **Connections**. How many tweets you can send per month depends on your plan.

## How to connect Twitter/X

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Connections**.
2. Find **Twitter/X** and click **Connect**.
3. Sign in to X (Twitter) when asked and approve the permissions.
4. You’ll return to Social0 with the account connected.

After that, when you create a post you can select your X account to receive it.

## What you can post

- **Text** - Plain text posts (tweets).
- **Image** - One or more images with an optional caption.
- **Video** - Video with optional caption.
- **Thread** - Multi-post threads (multiple tweets in a row).
- **Collection** - Multiple images or videos in one post (collection post).

X has its own character limit per tweet (280 for most accounts; higher for X Premium). Social0 will show you how much space you have as you type.

## Limits you’ll notice

- **Tweets per month** - Your plan sets a monthly limit (e.g. Starter: 200, Growth: 1,000; Pro: higher). We’ll show your usage on the Billing page.
- **Character limit** - 280 characters per tweet unless you have X Premium, which allows longer posts.

## Tips

- Use the thread post type when you want a multi-tweet thread; Social0 will post each part in order.
- If you have X Premium, we’ll use the higher character limit for that account.
