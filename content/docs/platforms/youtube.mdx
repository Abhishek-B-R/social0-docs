## Overview

You can connect your YouTube channel to Social0 and publish videos (with optional title and description). Connection is done from **Dashboard** → **Connections**. We only need permission to upload videos and read your channel info.

## How to connect YouTube

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Connections**.
2. Find **YouTube** and click **Connect**.
3. Sign in to Google/YouTube when asked and approve the permissions (upload and read).
4. You’ll return to Social0 with the channel connected.

When you create a **video** post, you can select your YouTube channel to receive it.

## What you can post

- **Video only** - YouTube in Social0 is for video uploads. Add a title and description in the post when you create it.

## Limits you’ll notice

- YouTube has its own limits on video length and file size. Very long or large videos may take a while to upload or process.
- We use your timezone and the time you set for scheduling; the video will be published at that time on YouTube.

## Tips

- When you pick “Video” as the post type and choose YouTube, fill in the title and description so the video looks right on your channel.
- If your connection ever stops working, disconnect and reconnect YouTube from Connections.
