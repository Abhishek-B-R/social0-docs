## Overview

You can connect your LinkedIn profile and company pages to Social0 and publish text, images, and videos. Connection is done from **Dashboard** → **Connections**. You can connect your personal profile and one or more company pages.

## How to connect LinkedIn

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Connections**.
2. Find **LinkedIn** and click **Connect**.
3. Sign in to LinkedIn when asked and approve the permissions.
4. You’ll return to Social0 with the account connected.

When you create a post, you can choose which LinkedIn account (profile or page) should receive it.

## What you can post

- **Text** - Plain text posts.
- **Image** - One or more images with an optional caption.
- **Video** - Video with optional caption.

## Tips

- If you manage company pages, connect each page you want to use. They’ll appear as separate accounts when you pick where to post.
- LinkedIn has its own length limits; the composer will reflect what’s supported.
