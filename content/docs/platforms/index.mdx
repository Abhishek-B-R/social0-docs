## Overview

Social0 works with the main social platforms you use for content. Connect any of them from **Dashboard** → **Connections**, then choose which accounts get each post when you create or schedule.

## Supported platforms

- **Twitter/X** - Text, image, video, thread, collection. Tweet limits per month depend on your plan.
- **Bluesky** - Text, image, video, thread.
- **LinkedIn** - Text, image, video (personal profile and company pages).
- **Threads** - Text, image, video, thread, collection.
- **Instagram** - Image, video, collection (connected via a linked Facebook Page).
- **Facebook** - Text, image, video (Pages only).
- **YouTube** - Video only.
- **TikTok** - Image, video. Posts may take a few minutes to show up.
- **Pinterest** - Image, video.

Each platform has its own limits (e.g. video length, image size). The create flow will guide you. For details on connecting and limits for a specific platform, use the links below.

## How to connect a platform

You need a **paid plan** to connect accounts; on the free tier you cannot connect any. Once you have a paid plan:

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Connections**.
2. Click **Connect** next to the platform you want.
3. Sign in to that platform when prompted and approve the permissions.
4. You’ll return to Social0 with the account connected.

## Tips

- You can connect multiple accounts for the same platform (e.g. two Twitter accounts) and choose which ones get each post.
- If a platform isn’t in the list, we don’t support it yet. Check back or contact support.
