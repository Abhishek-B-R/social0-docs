## Overview

Besides creating and publishing posts, Social0 lets you schedule for later, use a queue for consistent posting, upload multiple images or videos at once (bulk tools), and-on Growth and Pro plans-resurface or auto-plug posts. This section explains each feature in plain terms.

## Features at a glance

- **Scheduling** - Pick a date and time; Social0 publishes the post for you then. Available on all paid plans.
- **Queue** - Set up time slots (e.g. “every day at 9 AM”) and assign scheduled posts to them. Your posts go out in those windows.
- **Bulk tools** - Upload and schedule many images or videos in one go. Available on Growth and Pro.
- **Auto-repost** - Resurface (re-post or boost) and auto-plug (add a plug to high-performing posts). Available on Growth and Pro.

Use the links below for step-by-step guides.

## How to use scheduling

1. Create your post (Composer or Create).
2. When you choose when to publish, select **Schedule** instead of **Publish now**.
3. Pick the date and time (we use the timezone in your Settings).
4. Confirm. The post appears in your Posts list as **Scheduled** and will go out at that time.

## How to use the queue

1. Go to **Settings** → **Queue** and set up your slots (e.g. which days and times).
2. When you schedule a post, you can assign it to one of those slots instead of a fixed time.
3. The post will go out in that time window. Scheduled posts that are in a queue show as **Queued** in your Posts list.

## Tips

- Set your timezone in **Settings** → **Preferences** so scheduled times are correct.
- Bulk tools save time when you have many images or videos to post over several days.
