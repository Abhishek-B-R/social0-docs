# Scheduling

When creating a post, you can **Schedule** instead of **Publish now**. Pick a date and time (in your timezone from Settings). The post is saved with status **Scheduled** and will be published at that time (via a cron job that processes the queue).

You can also assign a scheduled post to a **queue slot** (see [Queue](/docs/features/queue)) so it goes out in a designated time window.
