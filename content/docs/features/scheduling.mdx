## Overview

Scheduling lets you set a date and time for a post to go out. You create the post, choose **Schedule** instead of **Publish now**, pick the date and time, and Social0 publishes it for you at that moment. All times use the timezone you set in Settings.

## How to schedule a post

1. Create your post in the Composer or via Create (text, image, video, thread, or collection).
2. Select the accounts that should receive it.
3. Click **Schedule** (instead of **Publish now**).
4. Choose the **date** and **time** in the calendar/time picker.
5. Optionally assign it to a **queue slot** if you use the queue (see [Queue](/docs/features/queue)).
6. Confirm. The post is saved and will be published at the time you set.

You’ll see it in your **Posts** list with a **Scheduled** label until it goes out.

## Where to see scheduled posts

- Go to **Dashboard** → **Posts** and look for posts marked **Scheduled**.  
- You can filter or open a post to change the time, edit the content, or cancel it.

## Tips

- Set your **timezone** in **Settings** → **Preferences** so “9 AM” is 9 AM where you are.
- You can edit a scheduled post anytime before it publishes. Open it from the Posts list and make your changes.

## Common questions

**Q: Can I change the time after I schedule?**  
A: Yes. Open the post from the Posts list and change the scheduled date or time.

**Q: What if I scheduled for the wrong time zone?**  
A: Update your timezone in **Settings** → **Preferences**, then edit the post’s scheduled time if needed.

**Q: Can I cancel a scheduled post?**  
A: Yes. Open the post and delete it, or remove the schedule so it stays as a draft.
