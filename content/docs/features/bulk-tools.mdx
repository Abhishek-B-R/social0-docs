# Bulk Tools

**Bulk tools** are available on **Growth** and **Pro** plans. From **Dashboard → More → Bulk tools** you can open:

- **Bulk Video Upload** — Upload and schedule multiple videos at once (video-capable platforms).
- **Bulk Image Upload** — Upload and schedule multiple images at once (image-capable platforms).

If your plan doesn’t allow bulk tools, you’re redirected to [Billing](https://social0.app/dashboard/billing?upgrade=1) with an upgrade prompt.
