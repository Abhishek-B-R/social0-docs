# Auto-Repost

**Resurface** and **auto-plug** are available on **Growth** and **Pro** plans:

- **Resurface** — Re-post or boost a post (e.g. on Twitter/X) on a schedule. Configure per post.
- **Auto-plug** — Automatically add a plug/comment to high-performing posts.

These options appear in the create/edit flow when your plan allows them (`allowResurface`, `allowAutoPlug`).
