## Overview

Auto-repost features let you **repost** posts (re-post or boost them on a schedule) and **auto-plug** (automatically add a plug or comment to posts that do well). Both are available on **Growth** and **Pro** plans. If you’re on Starter or Free, you won’t see these options until you upgrade.

## What is repost?

Repost lets you take a post you’ve already published and send it again (or boost it) on a schedule. For example, you publish a tweet today and set it to repost after 2–4 hours-since Twitter generally considers a tweet "dead" if it hasn’t received interaction in that timeframe, reposting can give it another chance to be seen and engaged with. You configure this when creating or editing a post.

## How to use repost

1. Create a post (or open one you’ve already published).
2. Look for **Auto-repost**. Turn it on.
3. Choose how often you want the post to be reposted: set the interval (in hours) and the number of times to repeat.
4. Save or publish. When the time comes, Social0 will repost it again to the accounts you chose.

You can also set repost from the post detail page: open the post and use the repost option there.

## What is auto-plug?

Auto-plug automatically adds a plug or comment to posts that perform well (e.g., get a lot of engagement). You turn it on per post. Social0 then applies the plug when the post meets the criteria you set. If the post never meets those criteria, it is considered inactive after a day and we move on.

## Where you’ll see these options

- When **creating** or **editing** a post, if your plan allows it you’ll see options for repost and/or auto-plug.

If you don’t see them, you’re likely on Starter or Free. Go to **Dashboard** → **Billing** to upgrade to Growth or Pro.

## Tips

- Use repost for your best posts so they get a second (or third) chance to perform better.

- Auto-plug is optional; turn it off if you prefer to add plugs yourself.

## Common questions

**Q: I don’t see repost or Auto-plug. Why?**  
A: They’re included on Growth and Pro plans. Upgrade from **Dashboard** → **Billing** to get them.

**Q: Can I choose which posts get auto-plug?**  
A: Yes, you can choose which posts get auto-plug by toggling it on or off for each individual post.
